Child Safety & Anti-CSAE Policy

Mewad Maheshwari Samaj Nadiad is committed to the safety and protection of children. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation (CSAE).

Last updated: 11/11/2025

Scope

This policy applies to all users of Mewad Maheshwari Samaj Nadiad, including account holders, content contributors, moderators, contractors, and partners. It covers any content or behavior that sexualizes, exploits, harms, or puts minors at risk.

Prohibited Content & Behaviour

  • Any sexual content involving minors (real or simulated).
  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or links to CSAM.
  • Grooming, sexual solicitation or enticement of minors.
  • Sharing images or personal data of minors for exploitative purposes.
  • Encouraging or facilitating sexual contact with a minor.

Prevention, Detection & Moderation

Automated Tools
We use automated detection systems such as hashing, pattern recognition, and filters to identify suspected CSAM and flagged content.

Human Review
Trained moderators review flagged content promptly and escalate clear violations to law enforcement when required by law.

Age-safety
We do not allow underage accounts and employ measures to detect and limit interactions that may put minors at risk.

Access Controls
We restrict sensitive features and employ reporting and blocking tools for users.

How to Report Suspected CSAE

If you encounter content, behaviour, or an account that may involve CSAE, please report it immediately using one of the channels below. Do not download, copy, or redistribute suspected CSAM.

  • In-app: Use the "Report" button available on content and user profiles.
  • Email: nadiadmaheshwarisamaj@gmail.com
  • Law enforcement: Contact your local law enforcement or national reporting center (e.g., NCMEC in the United States).

Reports are reviewed 24/7. Where required by law, we will preserve evidence and report to law enforcement or national hotlines.

Response & Enforcement

  • Content that violates this policy will be removed immediately.
  • Accounts found to be involved in CSAE will be suspended or terminated.
  • We will cooperate with law enforcement and may retain and provide logs, metadata and content as required.

Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable laws and regulations related to child protection, including but not limited to local laws, the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act (India), and international reporting obligations such as NCMEC.
